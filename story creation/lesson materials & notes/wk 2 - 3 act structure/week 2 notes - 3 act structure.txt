the shorter the story, the harder to work with story


structure of creating stories:
------------------------------
synopsis

outline: take the essentials

act structure:
act 1 -> 
	--> introduction: set up the characters
	--> inciting incident: something out of the ordinary 	happens
	--> plot 1: the problem for the characters
act 2 ->
	--> pinch 1: villain show their power
	--> midpoint: show the context of the story
	--> pinch 2: higher stakes
	--> plot 2: significant turning point to solve the 	problem
act 3 ->
	--> climax: final moment of conflict
	--> resolution: wrap up the story
	--> end


how to make interesting story:
------------------------------
- avoid convenient solutions
- show not tell
- develop protagonist
